If \(X\) is a binomial random variable for a binomial process involving \(n\) trials in which the probability of a success in each trial is \(p\text{,}\) then the mean and standard deviation of \(X\) are:

\begin{equation*} \text{Mean: } \mu = n \times p \qquad \text{Standard Deviation: } \sigma = \sqrt{n\times p\times q} \end{equation*}
