Here we need to use inclusion/exclusion. If we add the “van drivers”, 18 from (a), to those with “1-20 years of experience”, \(7+13+7 = 27\text{,}\) we double count those from (b) who both drive a van and have 1-20 years experience. Using inclusion/exclusion, our answer is \(18 + 27-13 = 32\text{.}\)
