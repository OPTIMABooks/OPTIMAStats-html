A sample space \(S\) contains two mutually exclusive events, \(A\) and \(B\) with probabilities \(P(A) = 0.6\) and \(P(B) = 0.2\text{.}\) Use this to answer the following questions.

  1. Which event is more likely to occur, \(A\) or \(B\text{?}\)

  2. What is the probability that either \(A\) or \(B\) will occur?

  3. What if you were told that \(C\) is another event in the sample space with \(P(C) = 7\text{?}\)
