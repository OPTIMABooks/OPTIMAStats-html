Since the population has a normal distribution, we can use the central limit theorem. However, we do not need it for the first question as we are selecting an individual from a normally distributed population. \begin{equation*} \sigma_{\overline W} = \frac{\sigma_W}{\sqrt{n}} = \frac{4.5}{\sqrt{10}} \approx 1.423\text{.} \end{equation*}
