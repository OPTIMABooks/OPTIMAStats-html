However, the trials are not independent and the probabilities change. To see this, note that on the second draw the probability of a success is either \(\frac{9}{19}\text{,}\) if we drew a red on the first draw, or \(\frac{10}{19}\text{,}\) if we drew a blue marble on the first draw. So the probabilities change and the new probabilities depend on what happened on the previous draw.
