We want to find \(P(W \gt 22)\text{.}\) Note that this is not a binomial approximation, so we do not apply a continuity correction! Instead, we compute the z-score for 22 and compute as shown.

\begin{align*} P(W \gt 22) \amp = P\left(\frac{W-\mu}{\sigma} \gt \frac{22-19.4}{4.5}\right)\\ \amp = P(Z \gt 0.58)\\ \amp = 1 - P(Z \lt 0.58)\\ \amp = 1 - 0.7190\\ \amp = 0.2810 \end{align*}

This is a fairly likely occurrence.
