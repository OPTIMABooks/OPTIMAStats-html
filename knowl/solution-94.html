Solution 2.3.30.1.

Based on the complement rule,

\begin{equation*} P(A\cup B) = 1 - P(\overline{A\cup B}) = 1 - 0.20 = 0.80\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then, using the general addition rule,

\begin{align*} P(A\cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A\cap B) \amp \Rightarrow 0.80 = 0.40 + 0.50 - P(A\cap B)\\ \amp \Rightarrow P(A\cap B) = 0.10. \end{align*}
