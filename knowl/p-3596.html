In this final chapter we will examine several additional hypothesis tests that are especially designed to help us make certain types of decisions. These are:

  • The Analysis of Variance Test.

    This test will help us determine if the means of more than two populations are equal or if at least one differs from the rest.

  • The Goodness-of-Fit Test.

    We use this test to see how well data from an observed frequency distribution fits a hypothesized distribution.

  • Tests of Independence and Homogenaity.

    Theses tests help us decide if observations that are classified using two variables in a contingency table indicatest that the varaiables are dependent.
