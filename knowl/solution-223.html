Solution 5.3.12.1.

Previously, we found the following hypotheses for this situation.

\begin{align*} H_0\amp:\ p \geq 0.10\\ H_A\amp:\ p \lt 0.10 \end{align*}

Under the null hypothesis assumption that \(p = 0.10\text{,}\) the test statistic is as shown below.

\begin{equation*} z_{\text{test}} = \frac{0.1375 - 0.1000}{\sqrt{\frac{(0.1)(0.9)}{80}}} \approx 1.12\text{.} \end{equation*}
