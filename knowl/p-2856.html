The \((1-\alpha)100\%\) confidence interval for the difference \(\mu_1 - \mu_2\) between means of independent populations is given by:

\begin{equation*} (\overline{x}_1 - \overline{x}_2) \pm z_{\alpha/2}\times \sqrt{\frac{\sigma_1^2}{n_1} + \frac{\sigma_2^2}{n_2}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
